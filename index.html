1. Choose an Image you want from your Computer

2. Press "RUN" so the Poster Generator can do his work

3. Press "RUN" again if you didn't like the result

4. Press "SAVE" to save the poster to your computer

Note:

- Use only image files like .png, .jpg or .gif! Also take small sized images, the Poster Generator works slowsly.

- The Poster looks now pixelated due perfomance issues. If you save it, it will be fine. Put the saved png in a new photoshop file with 300 dpi and you are print ready!
